INTAKE · FIT · ROUTING

The front door for founders.

Tell us the problem and the constraints. We’ll route you to the right lane or tell you honestly if there isn’t a fit.

FounderLink is the intake layer of Texas Movement. It is for founders and operators considering working with the ecosystem. It solves the problem of not knowing which door to use or whether there is a fit at all. It works in four steps: you send a brief, we assess fit, we route you or decline honestly, and you leave with one clear next step. It connects to every lane because routing you correctly is its entire job. No maze, no hard sell. Clarity and one next step.

Who it’s for

Founders and operators with a real problem and enough of a brief to assess—context, constraints, and what a good outcome looks like.

People who would rather be routed correctly than guess between multiple subdomains or lanes inside Texas Movement.

It is not for vague, no-context outreach. The clearer the brief, the faster and more accurate the routing.

How intake works

1. Send the brief

You send a short brief—context, constraints, and what a good outcome would look like. That is the entire ask.

2. We assess fit

The brief is read against what Texas Movement actually does. Fit is assessed on stakes, clarity, and lane alignment.

3. We route or decline

If there is a fit, you are routed into the correct lane. If there is not, you are told plainly rather than pulled into a misaligned engagement.

4. You leave with one next step

The output is one clear next step—an introduction to a lane, a suggestion, or a clean “not a fit” so you can move on confidently.

Frequently asked questions

What is FounderLink?

It is the intake layer of Texas Movement—the front door that scopes your problem and routes you to the right lane.

What should I send?

A short brief: context, constraints, and what a good outcome looks like. That is enough to assess fit and routing.

What happens after I send it?

It is assessed for fit. You are then routed to the right lane or told honestly if there is not a real fit with Texas Movement.

Is there a cost to intake?

No. Intake and the fit check are how a good engagement starts. Any scoped work happens only after fit is clear.

Can I go straight to a lane instead?

Yes. FounderLink exists so you do not have to guess, but you can enter a lane directly if you already know where your problem belongs.
